TERMS & SAFETY ▼
- All key generation runs natively on YOUR device. Nothing is sent over the network — this app makes no network calls at all.
- We never see, store, or have access to your private key.
- "SAVE .JSON" writes a standard Solana keypair file to your device. Keep it offline, never share it.
- Import it later with the solana-keygen CLI, or "Import private key" in Phantom, Backpack, or Solflare.
- Lost the file = lost the wallet. There is no recovery.
